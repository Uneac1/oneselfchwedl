问题Problem

商品上架需要人工长时间持续操作，商品信息读取、处理、填写和核对都依赖重复手动步骤。Product listing required long continuous manual work, with reading, processing, filling and checking all depending on repeated manual steps.

做法Approach

以 Excel 数据为入口，读取商品信息，加入伪随机处理和模拟人工执行，将上架 SOP 改造为工具流程。Used Excel as the data entry point, read product information, added pseudo-random handling and human-like execution, then converted the listing SOP into a tool flow.

结果Result

日常人工从约 6 小时缩短到约 30 分钟，主要工作变为维护 Excel 数据和检查异常状态。Daily manual work dropped from about 6 hours to about 30 minutes, mainly maintaining Excel data and checking exceptions.
